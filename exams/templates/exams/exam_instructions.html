Tilmaamaha Imtixaanka:

  1. Su’aalaha imtixaanku waxay ka kooban yihiin afar nooc: Dooro (MCQ), Run iyo Been (True/False), Su'aalo qoraal ah (Structured Questions), iyo Jiid oo dhig (Sequencing).
  2. Dooro (MCQ): Dooro hal jawaab oo sax ah kadib markaad si fiican u akhrido su’aasha.
  3. Run iyo Been (True/False): Dooro jawaabta saxda ah (Run ama Been).
  4. Su'aalaha qoraalka ah (Structured Questions):
    1. Part A → Qor magaca akoonka debit ka ah.
    2. Part B → Qor magaca akoonka credit ka ah
    3. Part C → Qor tirada lacagta saxda ah.
    Tusaale:

    Su’aal: Record depreciation of 2000

    Sida looga jawaabayo:
    Part A: Debit Depreciation Expense
    Tani waa account-ka debit-ka ah, sababtoo ah depreciation waa kharash (expense).

    Part B: Credit Accumulated Depreciation
    Tani waa account-ka credit-ka ah, sababtoo ah accumulated depreciation waa dhinaca credit-ka ee depreciation-ka la uruuriyo.

    Part C: Amount 2000
    Halkan waxaad ku qoraysaa tirada saxda ah ee lacagta, taas oo ah 2000.
  5. Jiid oo dhig (Sequencing): Jiid tallaabooyinka si ay u noqdaan nidaamka saxda ah.
  6. Wareegyada (Question Navigator):
    1. Wareegyada (1,2,3...) = dhammaan su’aalaha
    2. Midabada:
      1. Cagaar → laga jawaabay
      2. Cawl → lagama jawaabin
      3. Buluug → su’aasha hadda
    3. Riix lambar si aad ugu booddo su’aal kasta
    4. Haddii aad shaki qabto → ka bood oo dib ugu noqo
  7. Jawaabaha waa la kaydinayaa markaad u gudubto su’aasha xigta.
  8. Navigation (Socodka Su’aalaha):
    1. Previous: Riix batoonka Previous si aad dib ugu noqoto su’aashii hore una saxdo ama u eegto jawaabtaada.
    2. Next: Riix Next si aad ugu gudubto su’aasha xigta. Jawaabtaadana si toos ah ayaa loo kaydinayaa.
    3. Faa’iido: Waxaad si fudud ugu kala socon kartaa dhammaan su’aalaha adigoon lumin jawaabahaaga.
  9. La soco waqtiga kuu haray.
  10. Markaad dhamayso → riix Submit.

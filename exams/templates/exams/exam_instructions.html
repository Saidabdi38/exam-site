Tilmaamaha Imtixaanka:

  1. Su’aalaha imtixaanku waxay ka kooban yihiin afar nooc: Doorasho (MCQ), Run iyo Been (True and False), Su’aalaha Qoraalka (Structured Questions), iyo Jiid oo Dhig (Sequencing).
  2. Doorasho (MCQ): Dooro hal jawaab oo sax ah kadib markaad si fiican u akhrido su’aasha.
  3. Run iyo Been: Dooro jawaabta saxda ah (Run ama Been) adigoo fahmaya su’aasha.
  4. Su’aalaha Qoraalka (Structured Questions):
    1. Part A → Debit (Ku qor magaca account-ka debit ka ah.)
    2. Part B → Credit (Ku qor magaca account-ka credit ka ah.)
    3. Part C → Amount (Ku qor tirada lacagta saxda ah.)

    Hubi in Debit iyo Credit ay isku dheellitiran yihiin.

    Tusaale:

    Su’aal: Record depreciation of 2000

    Sida looga jawaabayo:
    Part A: Debit Depreciation Expense
    Tani waa account-ka debit-ka ah, sababtoo ah depreciation waa kharash (expense).

    Part B: Credit Accumulated Depreciation
    Tani waa account-ka credit-ka ah, sababtoo ah accumulated depreciation waa dhinaca credit-ka ee depreciation-ka la uruuriyo.

    Part C: Amount 2000
    Halkan waxaad ku qoraysaa tirada saxda ah ee lacagta, taas oo ah 2000.
  5. Jiid oo Dhig (Sequencing): Jiid tallaabooyinka si ay u noqdaan nidaamka saxda ah.
  6. Qaybta Hoose (Wareegyada):
    1. Wareegyada (1,2,3...) = dhammaan su’aalaha
    2. Midabada:
      1. Cagaar = waa laga jawaabay
      2. Cawl = lagama jawaabin
      3. Buluug = Su'aashadda hadda laga jawaabayo
    3. Wareeg gaar ah = su’aasha aad joogto
    4. Riix lambar si aad ugu booddo su’aal kasta
    5. Talo: Haddii aad shaki qabto → ka bood oo dib ugu noqo
    6. Navigation:
      1. Previous → su’aal hore
      2. Next → su’aal xigta
  7. Jawaabaha waa la kaydinayaa markaad u gudubto su’aasha xigta.
  8. Si joogto ah ula soco waqtiga.
  9. Markaad dhamayso → riix Submit.
